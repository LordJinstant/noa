Item 1: ABOUT OUR PATHNERSHIP WITH SIMCC.
About Women on the Higher Move Prayer & Empowerment Foundation ​At Women on the Higher Move Prayer & Empowerment Foundation, we are dedicated to transforming lives through a dual mission of compassionate humanitarian aid and academic excellence. We believe that by combining spiritual support with tangible empowerment, we can create sustainable change for the most vulnerable members of our society. ​Our Core Initiatives ​Empowering the Vulnerable: We stand in the gap for those in need by providing essential resources to the impoverished and helping helpless orphans return to school. We believe that education is the foundation of a brighter future and are committed to removing the barriers that keep children out of the classroom. ​Supporting Women: We focus on uplifting widows and single mothers by providing the tools, resources, and mentorship necessary to launch and sustain their own businesses. Our goal is to foster financial independence and security for these resilient women and their families. ​Academic Excellence: We are committed to shaping the next generation of thinkers. In partnership with SIMCC, we organize prestigious math contests and provide high-level coaching for students preparing for Olympiad exams, fostering a culture of critical thinking and competitive excellence. ​Our Vision We strive to create a community where every child has the opportunity to learn, and every woman has the means to thrive. Through prayer, empowerment, and education, we are building a path toward a higher, more prosperous move for everyone we serve. ​

NOA REPORT
  • Item 1:Why Smart Students Should Take on SIMCC Examinations The Singapore International Mastery Contests Center (SIMCC) has established itself as one of the most respected platforms for academic competitions worldwide. Its examinations are not simply tests of knowledge; they are opportunities for students to prove their intellectual strength, sharpen their problem‑solving skills, and position themselves as future scholars. A Test of True Intelligence Smart students thrive when challenged. SIMCC examinations are designed to push boundaries, requiring participants to think critically, apply logic, and innovate under pressure. Unlike routine classroom tests, these contests demand creativity and resilience—qualities that define outstanding scholars.
  • Item 2: Building a Scholar’s Profile For students aiming at scholarships, international recognition, or advanced academic opportunities, SIMCC contests provide a powerful credential. Success in these examinations demonstrates not only mastery of mathematics and science but also the ability to compete at a global level. Universities and scholarship boards value this kind of achievement because it reflects discipline, determination, and intellectual curiosity. Beyond Winning Writing SIMCC examinations is not only about medals or certificates. It is about testing one’s limits, learning from mistakes, and growing stronger with each challenge. Smart students understand that every problem solved is a step toward becoming a more capable thinker and a more confident leader. A Call to Action For Nigerian students and others worldwide, the message is clear: do not shy away from these examinations. Smart minds should embrace them as opportunities to showcase brilliance, to benchmark against international peers, and to prepare for the demands of higher education and professional life.

Singapore Hosts SIMCC’s Most Recent Examinations The Singapore International Mastery Contests Center (SIMCC) recently concluded its latest round of examinations, bringing together students from across the globe to compete in a series of rigorous academic challenges. Held in Singapore, these contests reaffirmed the country’s role as a hub for international education and intellectual exchange. Structure of the Examinations The examinations combined individual written tests with team‑based challenges, ensuring that participants were assessed not only on their mathematical knowledge but also on their ability to collaborate and think creatively. Students tackled Olympiad‑style problems designed to test logical reasoning, problem‑solving, and resilience. Global Participation This year’s examinations attracted participants from multiple continents, including Asia, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. The diversity of competitors highlighted SIMCC’s mission to create a truly international platform where young learners can benchmark their skills against global standards. Recognition and Awards Outstanding performers were honored with medals, certificates, and international recognition, achievements that strengthen academic portfolios and open doors to future opportunities in STEM fields. Beyond awards, the examinations provided students with invaluable experience in tackling complex problems under pressure. Impact The most recent SIMCC examinations in Singapore underscored the organization’s commitment to nurturing talent and fostering global connections. For many students, the event was more than a competition—it was a transformative experience that built confidence, encouraged innovation, and inspired future aspirations. ​
