# 🏆 Competitions Organized by SIMCC
SIMCC offers a wide range of international contests designed to challenge students and inspire excellence in mathematics, informatics, and other academic fields. These competitions provide opportunities for learners to benchmark themselves globally and cultivate problem‑solving skills.

# ✨ Major Competitions
Singapore International Math Olympiad Challenge (SIMOC)  
A prestigious global math competition that combines written tests with interactive team challenges, encouraging collaboration and creativity.

## Singapore Math Kangaroo Contest (SMKC)  
Part of the worldwide Kangaroo Math movement, this contest emphasizes logical thinking and problem‑solving over rote memorization.

## Singapore International Computational Olympiad (SINGA)  
Focused on computational thinking and informatics, SINGA helps students develop coding logic and algorithmic problem‑solving skills.

## Dr. CT Contest  
A competition designed to strengthen computational thinking, preparing students for future challenges in STEM fields.

## International Junior Math Olympiad (IJMO)  
Aimed at younger learners, IJMO introduces students to Olympiad‑style problem solving in a supportive environment.

## Vanda Science Global Finals  
A science competition that encourages inquiry, experimentation, and application of scientific concepts.

## Hippo English Olympiad  
An international English language contest that tests comprehension, grammar, and communication skills.

## Why These Contests Matter
Global Benchmarking: Students compete with peers from dozens of countries.

Skill Development: Enhances logical reasoning, creativity, and resilience.

Recognition: Winners gain international certificates, medals, and opportunities for further advancement.

Community: Builds a network of young scholars and educators worldwide.
